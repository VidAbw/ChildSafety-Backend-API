Place known person photos in this folder.

File naming = person label shown on the preview:
    parent.jpg        → "parent"
    nanny.jpg         → "nanny"
    grandma_jane.png  → "grandma_jane"

Tips:
- One face per photo, well-lit, looking roughly at the camera.
- JPG / PNG / JPEG supported.
- Restart the camera loop after adding/removing photos.
